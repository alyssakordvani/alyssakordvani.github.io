GAME DESIGN PROJECTS


QUACK SQUAD


Overview

  • 4 Heroes come together when a childhood friendship goes horribly south, leaving a Villain to seek out destruction on the world.
  • Players move around a map, collecting hero tokens, upgrading abilities, and taking control of districts to triumph in victory of the game.

My Contributions

  • I had full creative control of this game, as I designed the characters, gameplay, combat mechanics, and 3D models.
  • It was an interesting journey creating this game from start to finish and my favorite part of the whole process was being able to create 3D assets for my game. This was the first time I fully committed to the testing out my 3D modeling abilities on Maya.

    • MOOHAVEN

    • Be the first player to collect all 5 of the Celestial Bovine's Crests to win!

    My Contributions
